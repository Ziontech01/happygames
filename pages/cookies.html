🍪 Cookie Policy
Last updated: April 2025
🧒 Simple Summary

We only use essential storage to keep you logged in and remember your settings. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies. No cookie consent banner is needed because we only use what is strictly necessary for the site to work.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies and similar technologies (like browser storage) are small pieces of data saved on your device by a website. They help websites remember information about you between visits — for example, that you are logged in.

2. What We Use

Happy Games uses only essential storage. Here is exactly what we store and why:

Storage Type Purpose Essential?
Firebase Auth session token
Stored by Firebase in IndexedDB / localStorage		 Keeps you securely logged in so you don't have to enter your password on every page ✅ Yes
localStorage: hg_level Remembers your selected year/level (e.g. Year 3) so you don't have to choose it every time ✅ Yes
localStorage: hg_feelings_progress Saves your Feelings & Coping Quest badge progress locally ✅ Yes
localStorage: hg_sfx Remembers if you have turned sound effects on or off ✅ Yes
3. What We Do NOT Use
  • ❌ No advertising cookies
  • ❌ No third-party analytics (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • ❌ No marketing trackers or retargeting pixels
  • ❌ No social media integration cookies
  • ❌ No cross-site tracking
4. Firebase / Google

Firebase Authentication uses secure session tokens managed by Google Firebase. These are necessary for login to work correctly. Firebase may set its own internal storage as part of this authentication process.

You can read more about Firebase's data practices at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

5. Managing Your Storage

You can clear your browser's localStorage and cookies at any time through your browser settings. Doing so will:

  • Log you out of Happy Games
  • Reset your local year/level preference and sound setting

Your quiz scores and game history saved in Firestore will not be affected — those are stored in the cloud linked to your account.

6. Why No Cookie Banner?

Under UK and EU privacy law, a cookie consent banner is required when a website uses non-essential cookies (such as advertising or analytics). Because Happy Games only uses storage that is strictly necessary for the service to function, we do not currently require a consent banner.

If we ever add non-essential cookies or analytics, we will update this policy and add a proper consent mechanism before doing so.
