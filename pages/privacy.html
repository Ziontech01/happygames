🔒 Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2025
🧒 Child-Friendly Summary

In simple words: We only collect your username and a password so you can log in and save your game scores. We don't collect your real name, address, phone number or photos. We don't show you adverts. A parent or guardian can ask us to delete your account and scores at any time by emailing info@ziontechnologyltd.com.

1. Who We Are

Happy Games is a free online learning platform for children aged 4–11, developed and operated by Zion Technology LTD (Company No. 10265734), based in the UK.

Company website: www.ziontechnologyltd.co.uk

Contact: info@ziontechnologyltd.com

2. What Information We Collect
  • Account information: A username (letters, numbers, underscores — not your real name), a password (stored as a secure hash via Firebase Authentication), and a display name (the name you choose when signing up).
  • Game progress: Your quiz scores, game results, and the year/level you select. This is stored so you can see your history.
  • Technical data: Firebase may log standard technical data such as device type and IP address as part of its security and authentication service. We do not use this data for marketing.
  • We do NOT collect: real names (unless the child types one as their display name), email addresses, phone numbers, home addresses, or profile photos.
3. Why We Collect It
  • To let you log in and keep your account secure
  • To save and display your game scores and progress
  • To show your results on the leaderboard (display name only)
  • To let you pick up where you left off
4. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Firestore), a cloud service provided by Google LLC. Firebase stores data in secure data centres. You can read Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. How Long We Keep It

We keep your account and progress data for as long as you have an active account. If you request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days.

6. Who Can See Your Data
  • Only you can see your full results history.
  • The leaderboard shows your display name and scores only — no other personal information.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.
  • Firebase/Google processes data on our behalf as a data processor.
7. Parents & Guardians

Parents and guardians can:

  • Request to see what data we hold about their child
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of their child's account and all associated data

To exercise any of these rights, email info@ziontechnologyltd.com with the subject "Data Request — [Username]".

8. Cookies & Storage

We use browser storage (localStorage) only to save your year-level preference locally. Firebase Authentication uses secure session tokens to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

9. Children's Privacy

Happy Games is designed for children aged 4–11. We collect the minimum data needed to provide the service. Account creation should be supervised by a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Happy Games after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact

Email: info@ziontechnologyltd.com
