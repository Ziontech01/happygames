🛡️ Safety & Moderation
How we keep Happy Games safe for every child
🌟 Our Safety-First Promise

Happy Games is built with child safety as the absolute top priority. Every feature has been designed to protect children aged 4–11. This page explains the safety measures we have in place.

✅ Our Safety Commitments
✅ No open text chat in any game
✅ No user profile photos
✅ No real names required
✅ No third-party advertising
✅ No social media integration
✅ No in-app purchases
✅ Private rooms with code access only
✅ Room codes expire after inactivity
✅ Usernames: letters, numbers, underscores only
✅ Leaderboard shows display name only
🌐 Multiplayer Safety

Our multiplayer games (Chess, Tic-Tac-Toe, Memory, Matching, Ludo, Snakes & Ladders, and Spot the Difference) allow two players to connect online.

  • Private rooms only — Players join using a private 6-letter code. No random matchmaking.
  • No open chat — Players cannot type messages to each other. All interaction happens through gameplay.
  • Display name only — Players are shown only by the display name they chose (e.g. "HappyLion"). No real names, emails, or photos.
  • Auto-expiry — Rooms close automatically after a period of inactivity.

We recommend children only share room codes with friends they know personally.

👤 Usernames & Display Names

All usernames must follow our rules:

  • Letters, numbers, and underscores only (no spaces or special characters)
  • Minimum 3 characters
  • No offensive, inappropriate, or personal information (e.g. no real surnames, no phone numbers)

We monitor for inappropriate usernames and may suspend accounts that violate our rules. If you notice an inappropriate username, please report it to info@ziontechnologyltd.com.

🚨 Reporting Inappropriate Behaviour

If you experience or witness anything inappropriate on Happy Games, please report it immediately:

  • 📧 Email: info@ziontechnologyltd.com
  • 📌 Subject: Safety Report
  • Please include the username involved (if known) and a brief description of what happened

We aim to review all safety reports within 2 working days. For urgent concerns, please mark your subject as URGENT.

🏫 For Parents & Teachers
  • We recommend children aged 4–7 use Happy Games with adult supervision, especially for multiplayer games.
  • Teachers may use Happy Games in the classroom as a supplementary learning and engagement tool.
  • If you have safety concerns about any content or feature, contact info@ziontechnologyltd.com.

For a full overview of what the platform collects and how it works, see our Parent Guide.

🚫 Account Suspension

We reserve the right to suspend or permanently remove accounts that:

  • Use offensive or inappropriate usernames or display names
  • Attempt to misuse, exploit, or cheat on the platform
  • Behave in a way that makes the experience unsafe or unpleasant for others

Suspended accounts may appeal by emailing info@ziontechnologyltd.com.

📚 Content Safety

All quiz questions and game content on Happy Games are reviewed to be age-appropriate for children aged 4–11. Questions are based on the UK primary school curriculum. Topics include: English, Maths, Science, Grammar, Spelling, Money, Time, and General Knowledge.

If you find any content you believe is inappropriate or inaccurate, please report it to info@ziontechnologyltd.com with the subject Content Report.

📬 Contact for Safety Concerns

Email: info@ziontechnologyltd.com

Response time: within 2 working days (urgent reports prioritised).
